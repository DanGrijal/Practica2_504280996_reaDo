About the author

Dan Brown is an American author known for his bestselling novels, particularly those in the thriller and mystery genres. He was born on June 22, 1964, in Exeter, New Hampshire, USA. Dan Brown is perhaps best known for his Robert Langdon series, which features the Harvard University professor of symbology and iconology as the protagonist. His novels often blend history, art, science, and religion into intricate and suspenseful plots.

"May our philosophies keep pace with our technologies. May our compassion keep pace with our powers. And may love, not fear, be the engine of change."

~Dan Brown
