About the author

Carolina Andújar is an Argentine author known for her work in the horror and fantasy genres, particularly in the field of young adult literature. She was born on May 14, 1982, in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Andújar gained recognition for her debut novel, "La maldición de la Cabeza Reducida" (The Curse of the Shrunken Head), which was published in 2009.
