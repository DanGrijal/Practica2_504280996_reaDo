About the author

Tess Gerritsen is an American author known for her contributions to the crime thriller and medical thriller genres. She was born on June 12, 1953, in San Diego, California, USA. Tess Gerritsen's writing is characterized by its suspenseful plots, strong character development, and a blend of medical and forensic elements with crime and mystery.
